FAQ

Who can attend?

Any current high school student who wants learn to build things.

What types of activities will students do outside of coding?

Students will participate in team bonding activities, tech company tours, hands-on hacking, and more!

What do I need to bring?

You should bring a possitive attitude and a laptop. Laptops will be provided by request if you do not have access to one (contact us).

How long is each track?

Our first track will be a week-long, hour-and-a-half-per-day, learn-to-code-awesome-things marathon.

How much does Hack Camp cost?

It's free! We'll also be providing lunch. We'll require a $50 deposit refunded after Hack Camp is over.

How fun will it be?

Very.

When is this?

The first track begins June 1st.

But I don't know how to code?

Perfect! This is the perfect opportunity to learn! We'll have mentors available at all hours and are targetting the program towards people with little to no experience.

I already know how to code!

There's always more to learn—why not learn more surrounded by awesome makers and doers with similar interests?
